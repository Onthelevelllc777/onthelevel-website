About Us

Founded by Christopher Halladay, On The Level LLC leverages a deep foundation of practical experience, from carpenter to Construction Coordinator, offering unparalleled understanding of the industry's demands and challenges. Under Chris’s leadership, projects benefit from extensive practical knowledge, strategic insight, and a commitment to excellence.

We are problem-solvers dedicated to navigating industry exigencies with skill, innovation, and dedication. Our core team of experienced professionals, augmented by a trusted network of skilled artisans and specialists, allows us to scale efficiently, adapt swiftly, and meet deadlines without compromising quality. We uphold the highest standards of safety, quality, and creative integrity, ensuring projects exceed client expectations and leave a lasting impact.

We cater to a diverse clientele demanding excellence, precision, and innovative solutions in visual storytelling and experiential designs. We partner with these visionaries to bring their ambitious concepts to life.

Film and TV Producers: We are the first choice for directors, producers, designers, and art directors in the film and television industry. Our expertise lies in the meticulous development and construction of authentic, visually stunning sets and intricate, custom-fabricated props...

Museum Curators: As a dedicated team of subcontractors, we excel in exhibit development, creating captivating, durable, and meticulously crafted experiences...

Schools and Churches: We enhance theater productions and church events with custom stage pieces and faith-based set designs that inspire imagination and connection...

Event/Trade Show and Marketing Projects: We specialize in designing and executing dynamic event productions, trade show exhibits, and large-scale marketing projects...

Our Capabilities: From traditional Production Flats to camera-ready cinematic sets, immersive museum exhibits, and unique custom fabrications, we consistently transform ideas into tangible, impactful results.
