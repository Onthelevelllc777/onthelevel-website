About Us

Founded by Christopher Halladay, On The Level LLC leverages a deep foundation of practical experience, from carpenter to Construction Coordinator, offering unparalleled understanding of the industry's demands and challenges. Under Chris’s leadership, projects benefit from extensive practical knowledge, strategic insight, and a commitment to excellence.

We are problem-solvers dedicated to navigating industry exigencies with skill, innovation, and dedication. Our core team of experienced professionals, augmented by a trusted network of skilled artisans and specialists, allows us to scale efficiently, adapt swiftly, and meet deadlines without compromising quality. We uphold the highest standards of safety, quality, and creative integrity, ensuring projects exceed client expectations and leave a lasting impact.

We cater to a diverse clientele demanding excellence, precision, and innovative solutions in visual storytelling and experiential designs. We partner with these visionaries to bring their ambitious concepts to life. Our esteemed clients include:

Film and TV Producers

We are the first choice for any director, producer, designer, and art director in the film and television industry that we have had the privilege of working with. That is because our expertise lies in the meticulous development and construction of authentic, visually stunning sets and intricate, custom-fabricated props. Every project we undertake is executed with a profound understanding of the demanding requirements of cinematic and television production.

Our team possesses a deep-rooted knowledge of cinematic aesthetics, including the nuances of composition, color theory, and visual storytelling. We are also highly proficient in the art and science of lighting, ensuring that every element we create will seamlessly integrate with the desired mood and atmosphere. Furthermore, our experience extends to practical effects, where we skillfully craft elements that enhance realism and provide tangible impact on screen.

Crucially, we understand the complexities of on-set operational needs. This encompasses everything from efficient construction and breakdown to safety protocols and the logistical considerations of working within a fast-paced production environment. This comprehensive understanding makes us an invaluable asset to any production, as we are committed to bringing cinematic visions to life with unparalleled realism, creative ingenuity, and a dedication to seamless execution.

Museum Curators

As a dedicated team of subcontractors, we excel in the art of exhibit development, specializing in the creation of captivating, durable, and meticulously crafted experiences. Our passion lies in crafting exhibits that not only engage and educate but also stand as enduring testaments to their subjects. We are committed to upholding both historical accuracy and artistic integrity in every project, transforming ordinary spaces into immersive journeys of discovery.

Our expertise encompasses a comprehensive approach, from concept and design to fabrication and installation. We work closely with our clients to understand their vision, translating complex narratives into accessible and visually stunning displays. Whether for museums, cultural centers, or educational institutions, our exhibits are designed to inspire wonder, spark curiosity, and facilitate profound learning experiences for visitors of all ages.

Schools and Churches

We enhance theater productions with fundamental, innovative, and inspiring upgrades. Our expertise lies in crafting custom stage pieces, visually captivating and meticulously engineered for safety and durability, built to client specifications. These sets and props enhance storytelling, spark imagination, and deepen connection to the material. For churches, we construct sets that powerfully facilitate faith-based narratives like the Nativity or Resurrection, creating sacred and engaging atmospheres for deeper connection to faith.

Event, Trade Show & Marketing Projects

We offer comprehensive and strategic partnerships in designing and executing exceptional, highly functional, and memorable builds. Our expertise covers dynamic event productions, intricate trade show exhibits, and large-scale marketing projects for prominent companies. From concept to installation, we manage every aspect for a flawless experience. We specialize in unique, lasting marketing displays and high-level product exhibitions, ensuring seamless execution, logistical precision, and unforgettable products that captivate audiences. We blend stunning aesthetics, robust practicality, and meticulous attention to detail to exceed expectations and transform ideas into impactful realities.
