About

On The Level LLC is a creative construction company specializing in camera-ready sets and precision fabrication for film, television, exhibits, live events, and custom projects. Founded by Christopher Halladay (Founder), we bring extensive experience as a Construction Coordinator and Foreman across Hollywood features, network TV, museums, studios, and large-scale event builds. Our team scales with a trusted network of craftspeople to meet tight timelines without compromising safety, quality, or creativity.

Target Audience

We proudly serve museum curators, drama instructors, event planners, and Hollywood producers. Whether you need Hollywood flats, a camera-ready set, a full museum exhibit, or a custom fabrication that doesn’t yet exist, we make it possible.
